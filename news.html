Project Update
2026

Hampton Hills construction milestone

Our flagship Richmond, Kentucky cottage-style BTR community — 97 units on 22 acres — closes capital raise with site work beginning Spring 2026.

Market Expansion
2026

Lone Tree expands into Iowa market

Maple Heights in Norwalk, Iowa joins the pipeline — 100 townhome-style BTR units (Phase 1) within a 128-acre master plan in one of the Des Moines metro's fastest-growing suburbs.

Partnership
2025

Lane Aldrich joins as CEO of Capital Partners

With a background funding ~4,000 rental units representing over $1B in total development cost, Lane Aldrich brings institutional capital markets expertise as CEO of Lone Tree Capital Partners.

Builder Relations
2025

D.R. Horton takedown agreements executed

National builder takedown agreements executed across Redbud (120 units, KCK) and Magnolia Fields (118 units, Gardner) — validating market demand and creating built-in exit certainty.

Community
2025

Magnolia Fields advances in Gardner, Kansas

After collaborative engagement with Gardner city leadership, Magnolia Fields — 118 townhome and duplex units on 18 acres — moves into civil design with construction targeted for Summer 2026.

2025

Washington JV developments pass 160 units delivered

The Meadows (Sunnyside, 88 units) and Sunridge (Kennewick, 166 units) continue delivering units in partnership with North 44 Group — 163 units completed to date across the two Washington communities.
