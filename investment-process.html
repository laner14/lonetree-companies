Deal Lifecycle

From market selection to investor exit

Every Lone Tree project follows a disciplined six-phase process — designed to control risk, protect timelines, and deliver institutional-quality outcomes for our limited partners.

01
Market Selection
Proprietary analysis of employment, population growth, rent fundamentals & city leadership
02
Land Acquisition
City partnership first. Acquire sites aligned with community growth plans & zoning goals
03
Entitlement & Design
PUD approval, preliminary plat, civil engineering, and infrastructure design
04
Construction
Horizontal site work by Lone Tree Communities. Vertical build with national builder partners
05
Lease-Up
Ameritrue manages lease-up targeting 6+ units/month absorption to stabilization
06
Stabilize & Exit
Portfolio sale or refinance at stabilization. Target 4–5 year investor hold period
