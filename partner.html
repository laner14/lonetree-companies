For Cities & Municipalities

A development partner that listens first

We don't arrive with a finished plan and ask you to approve it. We engage city leadership early — before acquisition — to shape projects around community needs, zoning goals, and growth priorities. Then we design, finance, and build the housing that answers it.

  • Pre-development engagement before land acquisition
  • Community plans shaped by local zoning goals and growth priorities
  • Attainable rents calibrated to local workforce incomes ($55K–$95K HHI)
  • Market-rate housing — no subsidies, no LIHTC complexity
  • In-house property management for long-term community stewardship
  • Single point of contact from entitlement through operations

Common questions

What markets do you operate in?

We focus on high-growth secondary markets in Kentucky, Kansas, and Iowa, with expansion plans across the Midwest and Southeast. See our current projects →

What is "attainable housing"?

Market-rate housing for households earning $55K–$95K — no subsidies or tax credit complexity required. Learn more about our approach →

What is the typical investment structure?

Projects are structured as 506(b) SPVs with a 4–5 year horizon and $25–30M typical project size. View the full investment process →
