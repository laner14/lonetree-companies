About Lone Tree

Built on the belief that attainable housing is an investment thesis

We partner with cities, deploy institutional-quality capital, and build communities where working families can afford to live well — in the high-growth secondary markets most developers overlook.

Our Story

A platform built for what's missing

America's housing shortage isn't concentrated in Manhattan or San Francisco. It's in the secondary markets where household formation is strong, price-to-income ratios are favorable, and cooperative municipalities are eager for quality residential development.

Lone Tree was founded in 2020 to fill that gap — not with subsidized housing or tax credit complexity, but with market-rate communities designed around real local incomes and built through genuine city partnership. From a single land acquisition in Kentucky, we've grown into a vertically integrated, multi-state development platform with over $50M in land and development AUM.

Today, our integrated platform of four companies manages every phase of the development lifecycle — from demographic-driven site selection through construction, lease-up, and long-term property management — controlling a pipeline of 500+ acres and 2,500+ fully entitled residential lots across four states.

Investment Thesis

We go where the fundamentals lead

Lone Tree targets secondary markets with a specific profile: stable employment anchors, organic population growth, undersupplied rental housing, and city leadership that welcomes quality development. We don't chase speculation — we follow data to markets where working families need homes and the economics support disciplined returns.

Our thesis in one line: America's secondary markets are undersupplied by millions of attainable rental units. We build them — with city partnership, institutional discipline, and in-house execution — in the metros where the data says demand is most durable.

Our Values

What drives every decision

These aren't aspirational. They're operational — embedded in how we source, build, and manage.

🤝

City-First Development

Every project begins with municipal engagement. We listen to what communities need before we draft a plan. Our best projects are ones the city helped shape.

📐

Attainable by Design

We target the deepest segment of housing demand — working families priced out of homeownership but overqualified for subsidized housing. This demographic drives stable occupancy, recession-resilient cash flow, and genuine community alignment.

🔗

Integrated Accountability

Four entities covering the full lifecycle under one platform. One team responsible from raw land through stabilized operations.

📊

Institutional Discipline

Every deal underwritten to institutional standards. Transparent fee structures. Conservative basis. Disciplined capital deployment.

🏘️

Long-Term Stewardship

We don't just build and leave. Our in-house property management ensures these communities are maintained to the standard residents and cities expect.

🌱

Shared Value Creation

The best investments serve everyone. Cities get the housing they need. Residents get quality homes. Investors get disciplined returns. That alignment is our edge.

Our Structure

Built to control the full cycle — by design

Lone Tree Companies operates as a vertically integrated platform — meaning we develop, build, and manage every community in-house. This isn't a loose network of vendors. It's a single organization engineered to control quality, protect timelines, and eliminate the margin leakage that erodes investor returns.

For our limited partners, this structure means one point of accountability, full transparency into every phase of execution, and confidence that incentives are aligned from land acquisition through stabilized cash flow.
