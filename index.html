Investment Thesis

Attainable housing in markets that welcome development

Most institutional capital chases the same 15 metros. We target fully entitled, construction-ready sites in markets where household formation, attainable price points, and city alignment converge. This disciplined market selection places our BTR product directly in the path of the strongest demand drivers — delivering favorable basis, strong city support, and rents that serve the real workforce.
