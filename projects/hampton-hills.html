City Partnership

How Richmond shaped Hampton Hills

Our engagement with Richmond began 14 months before we acquired the Hampton Hills site. Through meetings with city planners, the economic development authority, and council members, we built a detailed understanding of Richmond's housing landscape — what was missing, who was underserved, and where new development would have the greatest community impact. Hampton Hills isn't a project we brought to Richmond. It's a project Richmond helped us design.

Market Thesis

Why Richmond, Kentucky

Richmond sits at the intersection of demographic growth, housing scarcity, and institutional demand. Located along the I-75 corridor between Lexington and Knoxville, this market benefits from Eastern Kentucky University's steady enrollment, Baptist Health's regional expansion, and a growing light manufacturing base. Existing rental stock is dated and undersupplied relative to household formation.
